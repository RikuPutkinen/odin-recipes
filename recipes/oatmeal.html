Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal with pumpkin seeds and chia seeds.

Ingredients

  • 1 dl of oat flakes
  • 4 dl of water
  • 1 tbsp of pumpkin seeds
  • 1 tbsp of chia seeds
  • salt

Steps

  1. Measure everything except salt into a kettle.
  2. Let it boil on low heat for about 10 minutes.
  3. Season with salt.
