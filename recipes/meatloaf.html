Meatloaf

Description

Tasty meatloaf.

Ingredients

  • 400 grams of minced meat
  • 1 egg
  • 1 dl of bread crumbs
  • 2 dl of sour cream
  • 1,5 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of black pepper
  • 0,5 tsp of white pepper

Steps

  1. Mix sour cream with bread crumbs and let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Add rest of the ingredients and mix.
  3. Form two meatloafs about eight inches long each on the baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the oven at 225 Celsius for 30 minutes.
