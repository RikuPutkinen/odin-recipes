Potatoes

Description

Potatoes roasted in the oven

Ingredients

  • 10 potatoes
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into blocks.
  2. Put the blocked potatoes on a baking sheet.
  3. Put oil on the potatoes and season them with salt and pepper
  4. Put the potatoes in the oven for 30 minutes at 225 Celcius heat.
